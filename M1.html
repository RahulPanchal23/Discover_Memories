Tentative Itinerary


Day 1 : Genting Highlands (Dinner)


Arrive at KUL Airport &  Proceed for Genting Highlands. Located approximately 45km from Kuala Lumpur 1,740m above sea level, surrounded by green tropical rainforest is Genting Highlands – the city never sleep home of Malaysia’s only Casino. Average temperature during the day is approximately 20C, however can drop to 15C in the evening and rainy days. Accessible by road from Kuala Lumpur as well as cable known as Genting Skyway from lower cable car station we take a 3.5km to Genting Highlands. First stop of the tour will at the 400million old Batu Caves, a massive limestone outcrop, it’s the focal point of Hindu festival of Thaipusam in Malaysia. Photo opportunity at a 42.7m high statue of Lord Murugan, the tallest Lord Murugan statue in the world. Continue the tour to Genting Highlands, on arrival at lower cable car station, board the Genting Skyway to the mountain peak. Subject to weather condition, this 3.4km journey takes approximately 15 minutes to reach the peak. On arrival, enjoy a sumptuous dinner at First world café and enjoy the attractions. Overnight at Genting Highland .


Day 2 : Kuala Lumpur City Tour (Breakfast)


After breakfast at your hotel , proceed to KUL. On arrival you will be met by our local representative in the arrival area situated outside the customs hall. Enroute enjoy a panoramic tour of Putrajaya, designed as an intelligent Cyber city and the new federal government administrative centre. This is a brand new planned city still in its development. Later Proceed for Kuala Lumpur city tour, our first stop will be at The King’s Palace gate for photo stop and visit National Monument and Chocolate Boutique outlet. We will also drive pass by the Parliament House, National Mosque and Moorish styled Railway Station. Photo stop at Supreme Court, Independence Square and PETRONAS Twin Tower. Check into your Hotel and the rest of the evening is free. Overnight at Kuala Lumpur.


Day 3 : Sunway Lagoon (Breakfast & Lunch)


After Breakfast , we depart on a Full day trip to Sunway Lagoon . Sunway Lagoon offers adults and children non-stop fun and entertainment – all in one place! Enjoy the ultimate theme park experience with over 80 attractions sprawled across 88 acres of land! Have a splashing good time at the Water Park before enjoying the fun and excitement of the Amusement Park rides with the little ones. Go wild and interact with the animals at the Wildlife Park and gear up for more action at the Extreme Park before facing your fears at Scream Park. Whatever your fantasy, Sunway Lagoon has it! Overnight at Kuala Lumpur.


Day 4 : Malacca city (Breakfast & Lunch)


Enjoy an American breakfast Check out from hotel. Proceed for Malacca City, travel approximately two hours south in a comfortable, air-conditioned motor coach for a full day of sightseeing in Malacca City on the Malay Peninsula's southwest coast.Begin with a visit to the Stadthuys, a historical landmark structure located in the heart of Malacca City in the Red Square. Then take in a panoramic view of Malacca City and the surrounding region from the top of St. Paul’s Hill, which features historic St. Paul’s Church, the oldest church building in Malaysia and Southeast Asia and part of the Malacca Museum Complex. Enjoy a traditional Malaysian lunch at a local eatery, then follow the guide to Jonker Street, the center street of Chinatown. Once renowned for its antique shops, Jonker Street now features clothing and craft outlets as well as restaurants.Finally, visit the A Famosa fortress, which is among the oldest surviving European architectural remains in southeast Asia, and stop at Cheng Hoon Teng, a Chinese temple practicing the Three Doctrinal Systems of Taoism, Confucianism and Buddhism. Overnight stay at Malacca.


Day 5 : Tanjong Sepang (Breakfast)


After breakfast check out from hotel & proceed for Tanjong Sepang. Check inn at Avani Sepang Goldcoast Resort (A Hidden Gem In Malaysia). There you can enjoy activities including banana boat, flying fish, donut boat, kayak, paddleboard, bungee trampoline, hydro gel blaster, and water ball & on road village tour at your own. Overnight stay in Tanjong Sepang.


Day 6 : Departure (Breakfast)


Enjoy an American breakfast at your hotel. Later today, proceed to the airport as this fantastic tour comes to an end. Return home with wonderful memories of your holiday with us.
