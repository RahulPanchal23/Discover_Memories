Tentative Itinerary


Day 1 : Genting Highlands (Breakfast & Dinner)


Arrive at KUL Airport &  Proceed for Genting Highlands. Located approximately 45km from Kuala Lumpur 1,740m above sea level, surrounded by green tropical rainforest is Genting Highlands – the city never sleep home of Malaysia’s only Casino. Average temperature during the day is approximately 20C, however can drop to 15C in the evening and rainy days. Accessible by road from Kuala Lumpur as well as cable known as Genting Skyway from lower cable car station we take a 3.5km to Genting Highlands. First stop of the tour will at the 400million old Batu Caves, a massive limestone outcrop, it’s the focal point of Hindu festival of Thaipusam in Malaysia. Photo opportunity at a 42.7m high statue of Lord Murugan, the tallest Lord Murugan statue in the world. Continue the tour to Genting Highlands, on arrival at lower cable car station, board the Genting Skyway to the mountain peak. Subject to weather condition, this 3.4km journey takes approximately 15 minutes to reach the peak. On arrival, enjoy a sumptuous dinner at First world café and enjoy the attractions. Overnight at Genting Highland .


Day 2 : Arrive Langkawi City tour (Breakfast)


After breakfast check out from Genting Hotel & depart for KUL Airport for Langkawi. On arrival in Langkawi, you will be welcomed by our representative and transferred to your hotel covering city tour of langkawi. Overnight at langkawi.


Day 3 :Langkawi Island Hopping Tour (Breakfast)


On today’s Island hopping experience you will embark on a boat trip to the various outlying islands off Langkawi island . Gliding through the clear water, the breathtaking view will fascinate and astound you! Enjoy a refreshing dip in a fresh water lake found on the mysterious and legendary island of the Pregnant Maiden. Legend holds that childless couples may have their dearest wish granted by taking a dip! Hop to the next island, Pulau Singa Besar, a sanctuary for wild life and for the adventurous. Tread the hundred meters long hanging bridge. Relax on the superfine sand or swim in the clear waters of Pulau Beras Basah before returning to the hotel. Later in the afternoon proceed for a fascinating tour that will take you through the urban and rural areas of Langkawi. . A sightseeing coach tour to various places of natural attractions, history & legends of Langkawi – Batik and Art Village, Dataran Lang or Eagle’s Square, rubber estates and Hindu Temple and the final stop will be Kuah Town. Overnight at langkawi.


Day 4 : Arrive in Kuala Lumpur Day free (Breakfast)


After breakfast at your hotel , proceed to KUL. On arrival you will be met by our local representative in the arrival area situated outside the customs hall. Day free for leisure & shopping at your own. Overnight at Kuala Lumpur.


Day 5 : Kuala Lumpur City Tour (Breakfast)


After breakfast at your hotel , proceed for a panoramic tour of Putrajaya, designed as an intelligent Cyber city and the new federal government administrative centre. This is a brand new planned city still in its development. Later Proceed for Kuala Lumpur city tour, our first stop will be at The King’s Palace gate for photo stop and visit National Monument and Chocolate Boutique outlet. We will also drive pass by the Parliament House, National Mosque and Moorish styled Railway Station. Photo stop at Supreme Court, Independence Square and PETRONAS Twin Tower. Check into your Hotel and the rest of the evening is free. Overnight at Kuala Lumpur.


Day Departure (Breakfast)


Enjoy an American breakfast at your hotel. Later today, proceed to the airport as this fantastic tour comes to an end. Return home with wonderful memories of your holiday with us.
