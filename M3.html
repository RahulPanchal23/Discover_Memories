Tentative Itinerary


Day 1 : Arrive Langkawi City tour (Breakfast)


Arrive at Langkawi Airport. On arrival in Langkawi, you will be welcomed by our representative and transferred to your hotel covering City Tour of Langkawi. Overnight at langkawi.


Day 2 : Langkawi Island Hopping Tour (Breakfast)


After breakfast, On today’s Island hopping experience you will embark on a boat trip to the various outlying islands off Langkawi island . Gliding through the clear water, the breathtaking view will fascinate and astound you! Enjoy a refreshing dip in a fresh water lake found on the mysterious and legendary island of the Pregnant Maiden. Legend holds that childless couples may have their dearest wish granted by taking a dip! Hop to the next island, Pulau Singa Besar, a sanctuary for wild life and for the adventurous. Tread the hundred meters long hanging bridge. Relax on the superfine sand or swim in the clear waters of Pulau Beras Basah before returning to the hotel. Later in the afternoon proceed for a fascinating tour that will take you through the urban and rural areas of Langkawi. . A sightseeing coach tour to various places of natural attractions, history & legends of Langkawi – Batik and Art Village, Dataran Lang or Eagle’s Square, rubber estates and Hindu Temple and the final stop will be Kuah Town. Overnight at langkawi.


Day 3 : Genting Highlands (Breakfast & Dinner)


After breakfast check out from KUL Hotel & we depart on a Full day trip to Sunway Lagoon . Sunway Lagoon offers adults and children non-stop fun and entertainment – all in one place! Enjoy the ultimate theme park experience with over 80 attractions sprawled across 88 acres of land! Have a splashing good time at the Water Park before enjoying the fun and excitement of the Amusement Park rides with the little ones. Go wild and interact with the animals at the Wildlife Park and gear up for more action at the Extreme Park before facing your fears at Scream Park. Whatever your fantasy, Sunway Lagoon has it. Check inn at Avani Sepang Goldcoast Resort (A Hidden Gem In Malaysia) & Overnight stay in Tanjong Sepang.


Day 4 : Kuala Lumpur City Tour (Breakfast)


After breakfast at your hotel , proceed to KUL. On arrival you will be met by our local representative in the arrival area situated outside the customs hall, proceed for a panoramic tour of Putrajaya, designed as an intelligent Cyber city and the new federal government administrative centre. This is a brand new planned city still in its development. Later Proceed for Kuala Lumpur city tour, our first stop will be at The King’s Palace gate for photo stop and visit National Monument and Chocolate Boutique outlet. We will also drive pass by the Parliament House, National Mosque and Moorish styled Railway Station. Photo stop at Supreme Court, Independence Square and PETRONAS Twin Tower. Check into your Hotel and the rest of the evening is free. Overnight at Kuala Lumpur.


Day 5 : Tanjong Sepang covering Sunway Lagoon (Breakfast & Lunch)


After breakfast check out from KUL Hotel & we depart on a Full day trip to Sunway Lagoon . Sunway Lagoon offers adults and children non-stop fun and entertainment – all in one place! Enjoy the ultimate theme park experience with over 80 attractions sprawled across 88 acres of land! Have a splashing good time at the Water Park before enjoying the fun and excitement of the Amusement Park rides with the little ones. Go wild and interact with the animals at the Wildlife Park and gear up for more action at the Extreme Park before facing your fears at Scream Park. Whatever your fantasy, Sunway Lagoon has it. Check inn at Avani Sepang Goldcoast Resort (A Hidden Gem In Malaysia) & Overnight stay in Tanjong Sepang.


Day 6: Tanjong Sepang (Breakfast)


After breakfast, day free for leisure & you can enjoy activities including banana boat, flying fish, donut boat, kayak, paddleboard, bungee trampoline, hydro gel blaster, and water ball & on road village tour at your own. Overnight stay in Tanjong Sepang.


Day 7 : Departure (Breakfast)


Enjoy an American breakfast at your hotel. Later today, proceed to the airport as this fantastic tour comes to an end. Return home with wonderful memories of your holiday with us.
