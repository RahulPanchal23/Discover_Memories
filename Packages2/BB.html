Bir Billing Paragliding

Nestled amongst the aesthetic natural bounties, Bir is a small village in Himachal Pradesh which is also known as the paragliding capital of India. Bir is famous for enthralling activities including trekking, paragliding, mountain biking, and hand gliding, giving you the adrenaline rush you’ve been looking for. For the ones looking for a thrill-filled vacation to challenge your inner adventurer, Bir won’t disappoint you at all. Bir has something for everyone; head to the Bir Road market to quench your shopping cravings, leisure walk along the lake, enjoy few hours at the Himalayan Film school, explore the cafes that serve divine local Tibetan dishes, and watch the tranquilising sight as the sun sets in the sky gleaming with different hues.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0th Day: Delhi to Bir
1st Day: Paragliding from Billing to Bir. Bir exploration and cafe hopping.
2nd Day: Trek to nearby waterfall. Departure for Delhi

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Delhi to bir


Leave Delhi at 7PM, Pick up from Kashmiri Gate/Majnu ka Tila (Day 0). Overnight journey to Bir in a semi-sleeper volvo bus or tempo traveler.


DAY 2 : paragliding | Bir exploration


Reach Bir in the Morning & Check in to camps/hotel, freshen-up. Drive to Billing for the most awaited paragliding session. Enjoy the best paragliding in India. After this Adventurous ride, explore the local town of Bir. Visit famous cafes of Bir. Later return to the campsite/Hotel. In the evening, enjoy warm bonfire and dinner. Overnight stay in camps/hotel.


DAY 3 :  Local Sightseeing | Departure


Wake up and get mesmerized by the breathtaking sunrise. Post breakfast, trek to the near by waterfall. Enjoy the dense forest trail and the waterfall. Lunch will be served. After this Visit famous monasteries and tea garden of Bir or Famous Baijnath Temple and Palpung Sherabling Monastery (by own). In Evening get ready for departure. Reach Delhi the next morning with Beautiful memories.
