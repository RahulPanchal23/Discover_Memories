Tentative Itinerary


Day 1 : IPOH Perak


Arrive at KUL Airport, On arrival you will be met by our local representative in the arrival area situated outside the customs hall, proceed for Ipoh Perak. Located approximately 245km from Kuala Lumpur. Arrive at hotel, day free for leisure. Overnight stay at Ipoh


Day 2 : IPOH Perak Lost World Of Tambun (Breakfast)


After breakfast proceed for Lost World of Tambun, a famous theme park & jungle adventure, where you’ll enjoy all-day access to a series of seven themed attractions that range from relaxing to thrilling. Pre-booking admission to the Lost World of Tambun ensures a hassle-free experience upon arrival, so you can get straight to the amusement park, water park, wild animals, tin valley, tiger valley and hot springs that are surrounded by lush, tropical vegetation. Later proceed for hotel & overnight stay at Ipoh.


Day 3 : Genting Highlands (Breakfast & Dinner)


After Breakfast Proceed for Genting Highlands. Day free for leisure. Enjoy Dinner at Genting coffee terrace. Overnight at Genting Highland .


Day 4 :Arrive in Kuala Lumpur en-route Genting Tour (Breakfast)


After breakfast & Proceed for Kuala Lumpur. Located approximately 45km from Genting Highlands en-route covering Genting tour. Enjoy scenic drive passing through the hillsides of Malaysia and see local villages on the way. Travellers can choose to stop at Batu Caves and Genting Strawberry leisure Farm (own expenses) before or after Genting Highlands trip. we will stop at the Skyway Cable Car and take a ride on one of the longest and fastest gondola lifts in Southeast Asia to the summit of Genting Highlands. Have a breathtaking experience and fabulous natural view of the mountain and forest. Like watching the forest from the sky. Later arrive at Kuala Lumpur. Overnight at Kuala Lumpur .


Day 5 : Kuala Lumpur City Tour (Breakfast)


After breakfast at your hotel , proceed for a panoramic tour of Putrajaya, designed as an intelligent Cyber city and the new federal government administrative centre. This is a brand new planned city still in its development. Later Proceed for Kuala Lumpur city tour, our first stop will be at The King’s Palace gate for photo stop and visit National Monument and Chocolate Boutique outlet. We will also drive pass by the Parliament House, National Mosque and Moorish styled Railway Station. Photo stop at Supreme Court, Independence Square and PETRONAS Twin Tower. Check into your Hotel and the rest of the evening is free. Overnight at Kuala Lumpur.


Day6 : Departure (Breakfast)


Enjoy an American breakfast at your hotel. Later today, proceed to the airport as this fantastic tour comes to an end. Return home with wonderful memories of your holiday with us.
